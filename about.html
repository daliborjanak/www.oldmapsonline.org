The search engine for historical map

OldMapsOnline was developed with a love of history and heritage of old maps, and thanks to all participants, this site now lists over half a million historical maps. The project was created in 2012 through a collaboration between Klokan Technologies GmbH, Switzerland and The University of Portsmouth, UK.

Technology

The website is created by Klokan Technologies GmbH, specialists in online map publishing and in applications of open-source software. Our aim is to demonstrate a combination of tools for publishing historical maps with a focus on their easy accessibility by the general public. If you wish to include these technologies for other purposes, such as in National or European development projects, we can share our expertise and help play a part. Please contact us on our website.

Content

Since 2012, OldMapsOnline.org has indexed over half a million maps. This is only thanks to the archives and libraries that were open to the idea and provided their online content. All new participants are warmly welcomed.
